Game Rules
The familiar game of Rock, Paper, Scissors is played like this: at the same time, you and our esteemed computer will display one of three symbols: a rock, paper, or scissors. A rock beats scissors, scissors beat paper by cutting it, and paper beats rock by covering it. First to five wins. Naturally, whenever one player wins, the other loses.
